Programs

Our intelligent healthcare dashboard is designed to assist decision makers in monitoring of the following healthcare programs.

1

Expanded Program on Immunization (EPI)

This program was launched in Pakistan in 1978 to protect children by immunizing them against Childhood TB, Poliomyelitis, Diphtheria, Tetanus and Measles. Later, new vaccines e.g. Hepatitis B, Influenza, Pneumococcal and Polio vaccine were introduced in 2002, 2009 and 2012, and 2015.

2

Tuberculosis Program

(TBP)

Pakistan has the sixth largest population in the world and has the fifth greatest burden of TB. With an estimated 27,000 drug resistant TB cases each year Pakistan also ranks 6th for drug resistant TB among the 30 high burden countries. A total of 369,548 people were notified as having TB in 2018.

3

Lady Health Worker Program (LHWP)

Lady Health Worker Program is the most important community based programme of Pakistan having more than 92,000 female health workers across the country providing community based PHC services at the door step of the communities in rural areas and urban slums.
